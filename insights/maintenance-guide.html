The Maintenance Guide: Keeping Your Glass Partitions Crystal Clear

April 23, 2024 4 min read

Premium glass partitions are a long-term investment. While they are built to last, a small amount of regular care ensures they look as stunning as the day they were installed. Here is your definitive maintenance checklist.

Regular Cleaning Protocols

Dust and fingerprints are the enemies of transparency. Use a lint-free microfiber cloth and a pH-neutral glass cleaner. Avoid abrasive sponges or harsh chemicals that can scratch the protective coatings or damage the aluminum profiles.

Hardware Inspection

The moving partshinges, floor springs, and handlesare the heart of your partition system. Every 6 months, check for smooth operation. If a door starts to 'catch' or sag, it usually requires a simple adjustment of the hydraulic floor spring. Ignoring this can lead to glass misalignment.

Gasket and Seal Check

Over time, the clear gaskets between glass panels can attract dust. Wipe them down with a damp cloth to maintain the 'frameless' look. Check for any gaps in the silicone seals at the basethese are crucial for maintaining the acoustic integrity of the room.

Hardware Lubrication

High-traffic doors in Bangalore offices take a beating. A tiny drop of silicone-based lubricant on the pivot points every year will prevent squeaking and ensure the door closes with the satisfying 'click' that signifies high-end execution.
