The Execution Roadmap: From Site Measurement to Final Installation

April 23, 2024 5 min read

At Meaven Designs, we believe that 'premium' is not just a lookit's a process. Here is how we ensure zero-error execution for every glass project, from a single cabin to a 50,000 sqft coworking floor.

Phase 1: Precision Laser Measurement

The biggest cause of failure in glass projects is human error during measurement. Our team uses high-precision 3D laser scanners to map the site. Because glass cannot be recut once it's toughened, we measure twice to ensure we only cut once.

Phase 2: Technical Drawing & Approval

We provide detailed shop drawings for every project. This includes every profile, hinge, and glass edge. This ensures that the client and the design team are perfectly aligned before a single panel of glass is ordered from the fabrication plant.

Phase 3: Fabrication Excellence

We partner with the top glass processors in India. Our quality control team inspects every panel for edge quality, flatness, and optical clarity. We don't accept 'industry standard'we demand Meaven standard.

Phase 4: Specialized Installation

Installation is where the magic happens. Our in-house teams are trained specifically in high-end glass handling. We use specialized suction lifts and laser levels to ensure every panel is perfectly vertical and every door is perfectly balanced.
