The Origin

Started in Bangalore.
Focused on execution.

Meaven Designs was founded in 2019 in Bangalore, Bangalore — not in a corner office, but in the middle of fabrication floors and construction sites. From day one, the fundamental belief was simple: the Indian commercial interiors market needed an execution partner, not another middleman.

Then 2020 happened. The lockdown shut down sites, stalled projects, and forced a hard reset. But instead of walking away, Meaven came back in 2021 with a sharper focus — commercial glass execution for offices that needed precision, accountability, and speed.

Today, Meaven is a bootstrapped, founder-run company that manages glass partition, facade, and fit-out projects end-to-end for coworking operators, managed office providers, and design-and-build firms across Bangalore.
